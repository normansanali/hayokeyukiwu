Cam Newton is a prominent American football quarterback. Cam Newton now plays for the New England Patriots of the NFL since 2020. He has been professionally active in this sport since 2011.

Early Life

Cam Newton was born on May 11, 1989, and he is currently 34 years old. Cam hails from Atlanta, Georgia, and his zodiac sign is Taurus. Likewise, his full name is Cameron Jerrell Newton. Talking about his family, his parents are his mother Jackie, and his father Cecil Newton Sr. His father was a safety for the 1983 Dallas Cowboys and 1984 Buffalo Bills.

Likewise, Cam has an elder brother named Cecil Newton Jr. His brother played center for the Jacksonville Jaguars. Moreover, Cam also has a younger brother named Caylin. Caylin played quarterback for Howard Bison. It was back in the mid-2010s.

Educational Institutions and Football

From a young age, Cam was a talented sportsman. He played baseball and basketball as a youth. However, later on, Cam developed a fear of being hit by a pitch in baseball. Likewise, Cam also couldn’t avoid foul trouble on the basketball court. Therefore, he stopped playing baseball at 14 years old and also quit basketball shortly after attending high school. In the year 2015, Cam Newton graduated from Auburn University. He graduated with a degree in sociology.

Previously, Cam attended Westlake High School in Atlanta. There, Cam played for the high school’s football team. With 2,500 yards of passes, 23 touchdowns, 638 yards running, and nine touchdowns at the young age of 16 years, Cam Newton began to gain the attention of major college programs.

In his high school senior, Rivals.com rated Cam as a five-star prospect, the #2 dual-threat quarterback in the nation, the 14th quarterback, and the 28th player overall. The young player also received scholarship offers from various places. This includes Florida, Maryland, Georgia, Oklahoma, Ole Miss, Mississippi State, and Virginia Tech. Later on, Cam Newton committed to the University of Florida. This was back during the beginning of his senior year. At the time, Cam was a part of the top-rated recruiting class in the country for the year 2007.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Cam Newton is a famous football quarterback. He plays for the NFL’s New England Patriots. Before joining this team, Cam played nine seasons with the Carolina Panthers. His previous team, i.e. Carolina Panthers, selected Cam first overall in the 2011 NFL Draft.

Moreover, Cam had college football stints at Florida and Blinn. This was before joining Auburn. Auburn is where Cam won the Bowl Champion Series National Championship and Heisman Trophy in the year 2010. Newton is nicknamed “Super Cam” and the quarterback is the NFL career leader in quarterback rushing touchdowns at 70.

Likewise, Cam made an impact in his first season. During this season, Cam set the rookie records for passing and rushing yards by a quarterback. Eventually, Cam won the NFL Offensive, Rookie of the Year award, in the process. Moreover, Cam became the league’s first rookie quarterback to throw for 4,000 yards in a season. Similarly, Cam also was the first to throw for 400 yards in his NFL debut.

More Details

Similarly, the quarterback also set the season record for quarterback rushing touchdowns at the age of 14. Between the years 2013 and 2017, Cam led the Panthers to three division titles as well as four playoff appearances. The quarterback’s most successful season came in the year 2015. This was back when they named Cam “Most Valuable Player” (MVP). At the same time, Cam also helped the Carolina Panthers obtain a franchise-best 15–1 record en route to a Super Bowl appearance in Super Bowl 50.

Furthermore, Cam is also one of four African-American quarterbacks to win NFL MVP. He was also the first African-American quarterback to solely receive this award/title. Following his MVP campaign, the Atlanta-born quarterback struggled with injuries. His then-team, the Panthers made the playoffs only once in their subsequent four seasons.

Business ventures

In the year 2013, Newton partnered with Southern department store chain “Belk”. This was on his clothing line called MADE by Newton. Further, in May 2019, the quarterback’s cigar bar and restaurant named “Fellowship” formally opened at Olympic Park Drive Dr. This is located near Mercedes-Benz Stadium in downtown Atlanta.

Along with his commitment to football, Cam also participates in motivational speaking. The quarterback also has a foundation by his name that is dedicated to “enhancing the lives of youth by addressing their educational, physical, and social needs”.

Newton was a self-proclaimed pescetarian. He turned vegan in March 2019. Following this change in his diet, a few dietitians claimed that this may have delayed Cam’s injury recovery the following season. Moreover, Cam is a Christian and was seen speaking about his faith after winning the 2011 BCS National Championship Game. He said:

“It’s just a God thing. I thank God every single day. I’m just His instrument and He’s using me on a consistent basis daily.”

Cam met with a car crash on December 9, 2014. This was in Charlotte where Cam’s vehicle flipped and he consequently suffered two back fractures. Moreover, the quarterback’s daily routine during the game season involves waking up at 4:20 a.m., leaving the house at 4:30 a.m., and finally sleeping at 11:30 p.m.
