The Most Opulent Purchases Conor McGregor Has Made Recently

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Conor McGregor’s meteoric rise within the world of MMA has been an unprecedented rollercoaster ride. Quickly becoming the face of modern MMA, the “Notorious” one would see financial situation reflect his superstar status. McGregor has gone from highest paid UFC fighter to one of the highest paid athletes in the world.

From luxurious cars to thriving businesses, Conor likes to flash his wealth whenever he sees fit. And with his ever-expanding wealth comes increasingly extravagant purchases. McGregor’s grandiose spending has become as notorious and the man himself. The Irish southpaw’s lavish appetite and impulse buying complements perfectly the larger than life persona of the former two-division champion.

7 Panthere De Cartier Necklace: $18,400

“Mystic Mac” has always had an eye for opulent jewelry. The newest arrival to his vast collection of extravagant and ornate pieces is the Panther. The 18K golden beast growls with the ferocity, encapsulating McGregor's fiery Irish personality. With Tsavorite Garnets, that glare green as the Emerald Isles. Complete with an Onyx nose, this Panther is as stylish and graceful as its real life counterpart. Though not the flashiest piece in his collection, the Cartier panther is definitely the most intimidating. Conor is one of the fiercest combat athletes in the world, wit's only fitting he has a necklace to match.

6 Jacob And Co. Astronomia Casino 'Roulette' Watch: $620,000

This Jacob and Co. design is an astounding piece of intricate craftsmanship. The Astronomia Casino embodies the gambling spirit, with the game table faced backing encircled in a brilliant gold roulette wheel. A sapphire Earth chases a diamond Moon, all within a crystal dome. McGregor took to Instagram to flash the elaborate poolside, likely while sipping a tall glass of Proper Twelve. Though not an original piece (Drake owns the same watch), the $620,000 USD time piece is no doubt a welcomed addition to his rapidly expanding collection of fine time pieces.

5 Latest Shoes And Casual Wear

The stylish Irishman is not all silk and fine suits. Conor has been known to shed the elegant three-pieces and slip into casual wear. The caveat being that this is McGregor is never fully casual. Hitting the street in D&G calfskin nappa Portfolio sneakers, The blunt toe sneakers help "Notorious" with his billionaire strut. McGregor has also added a pair of Virgil Abloh's Louis Vuitton sneakers (in Irish green, of course) to his formidable army of sneakers. Never neglecting any body part, Conor finishes out the day with an Irish green canvas baseball cap by D&G. Few and far between are the casual days of the fiery mixed martial artist, but style is always a staple despite whatever mood he happens to be in.

4 D&G Swim Trunks: $385

Whether at the beach, lounging around the house, or breaking in a good sweat, Conor is always committed to doing it in style. What these tiny shorts lack in flash, they make up for in bold color. McGregor's been sporting these a lot lately and have become his go-to shorts of choice. After his recent loss to Dustin Poirier, and an adrenaline induced tirade toward his opponent; Conor shows all that his fighting spirit is alive, well and neon-lime green.

3 The Black Forge Inn

Owning a Whiskey company seemingly wasn’t enough for the Irishman, as he saw fit to purchase The Black Forge Inn in 2019. The tasteful interior is contrasted by the Irishman’s tattoo adorning the wall. Paying 2 million pounds for the establishment and with plans of expansion, Conor is set to make his name within the world of food and drink. With the recent sale of his majority stake in Proper No. Twelve, Conor has an additional $600,000 to his growing empire, perfect for his ambitious plans of international pub expansion.

2 Versace Tresor De La Mer Print Silk Shirt: $1,650

One word: busy. McGregor manages to make outlandish statements not only with his mouth, but with his choice of outfits. This silk button up screams fun, frivolity and ocean love. Adorned with various sea life and ocean blue silk, the Irishman has made it a point to let everyone know that you can be a fan of the sea while being all about style. The "Notorious One" continues to add flamboyant articles to his expansive wardrobe and this silk Versace doesn't have to try hard to stand out.

1 The Lamborghini Yacht: $3.4 Million

The epitome of excess, the $3.4 million USD yacht is almost as grandiose as the man himself. The yacht’s eye-catching, nostalgic exterior and 4000 horsepower ensures that even at sea, McGregor sports his trademark flash. McGregorFast now extends to the open water as the Irishman speeds through the water in style. The luxurious vessel crafted by Lamborghini is one of only 63, making it one of if not THE most cherished object within the Irish southpaw’s collection of outrageous purchases.
