Beyonce Sent The Best Christmas Card

Publish date: 2024-09-21

Media Platforms Design Team

Putting all cheesy Christmas cards to shame, Beyonce's 2014 holiday card is nothing less than what you'd expect. Capturing a moment from her dancing filled "7/11" music video, Bey chose a photo of her jumping out of a box under the tree. Because what gift could really be better than the gift of Bey? Celebrating both Christmas and the New Year, Beyonce wears an "All I want for Christmas is you" sweater styled with a pair of '2015' shades in her card that was reportedly signed, "Happy Holidays Love, Beyonce."
