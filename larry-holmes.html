Top Facts about Larry Holmes

  • Larry Holmes was born on November 3, 1949 in Cuthbert, Georgia.
  • He held the WBC heavyweight title from 1978 to 1983.
  • Holmes had a professional record of 69 wins and only 6 losses.
  • He is considered one of the greatest heavyweights of all time.
  • Holmes fought Muhammad Ali in his last ever fight in 1980.
  • He retired from boxing in 2002 at the age of 52.
  • Holmes was known for his powerful left jab and footwork.
  • He was inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame in 2008.
  • Holmes also worked as a commentator for HBO Sports after retiring.
  • His nickname was “The Easton Assassin” due to his hometown and fighting style.
