Hayley Erin is an American actress. She first appeared in eight episodes of the sketch comedy series MADtv from 2003 to 2005. She later made a one-episode role in The District, as well as appeared as the young Emily in Emily’s Reasons Why Not. Later in the 2000s, she became known for her role as Abby Newman in The Young and the Restless. After her recurring roles in Melissa & Joey and Austin & Ally, she starred as Kiki Jerome in General Hospital from 2015 to 2018. She landed a starring role in the upcoming television series Pretty Little Liars: The Perfectionists, set to premiere in 2019.
