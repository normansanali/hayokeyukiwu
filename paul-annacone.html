Biography

Paul Annacone was born in New York on March 20, 1963. Renowned tennis coach and former player who has coached some of the world’s top players, including Roger Federer and Pete Sampras . As a player, he won events like the 1988 Volvo International. He won the 1987 Japan Open Tennis Championships with doubles partner Kevin Curren. He went undefeated as a player at Shoreham-Wading River High School.
