Nigerians Seek For Justice After A Young Man Accused Of Rape Commits Suicide(+Details)

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Nigerians are seeking for justice after a young man accused of rape allegedly committed suicide.

The young Nigerian man whose name is given as Izu Madubueze is currently trending on Twitter after he took his life when a Twitter influencer names Nanichi Anese published his name and other names as men accused of rape.

In the series of tweets available to GhBase.com, Izu apparently reached out to Nani and asked to know who he raped but she didn’t have a good answer, instead she claimed the accuser wanted to remain anonymous. She later said the alleged victim said it was a “non physical sexual assault”

After Izu’s chats with Nani were leaked on Twitter, she deactivated her account following his death.

Izu’s last post on Twitter read; ” Oh if you are reading this I’m dead”. This post indicated that he had indeed committed suicide.

Nigerians are asking the appropriate authorities to do a proper investigation and seek justice for Izu.
