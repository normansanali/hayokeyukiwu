Who is Manuel Neuer?

Manuel Neuer is a well-known professional soccer player who plays for the German team. He has played as Bayern Munich’s goalkeeper and the German national team.

Because of his great strength, shot-stopping abilities, command, and control he is also known as a “sweeper-keeper.”

Who are the parents of Manuel Neuer?

Manuel was born on 27 March 1986 in Gelsenkirchen, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany.

His father’s name is Peter Neuer and his mother’s name is Marita Neue. He has a brother named Marcel Neuer. He does not have any sisters.

Manuel Neuer: Early Professional life and Career

Manuel Neuer first started his career playing as a professional footballer with Schalke. He started firstly with his amateur skills but his talent was sooner seen and made him go through every age group barrier.

Then he signed a professional contract with the club in 2005, but couldn’t make his first appearance on the field in his first season.

He got a chance after that to play in the second season and performed well there.

In the 2007-08 season, Manuel started playing for his team in the UEFA Champions League. In the penalty shootout round against Porto, he made the saves and led his team straight into the quarterfinals.

As he has shown the best performance in the league, he was nominated for the UEFA Club Goalkeeper of the Year award.

Though his team somehow lost the match. He played in 50 games in the season for his team and emerged as a new star.

His work inspired everyone and in 2010-11, he was given the duties of being the captain for Schalke.

He leads them so nicely and proved himself as a good skipper by leading his team straight into the Champions League Semi-final.

In 2011, he announced that his contract with Schalke was approaching its end and he isn’t willing to extend it further, for which he received a lot of backlash from Schalke fans.

Manuel became the second most expensive goalkeeper after some time and the first was Bayern Munich for a whopping 22 Million German Euro.

In a match against Real Madrid in 2012, he teaches and manages Kaka and Ronaldo to score penalty goals.

They as motivated by Neuer’s score and they moved their team into the semi-final of the championship league.

In the finals, They performed well but couldn’t win against Chelsea. Manuel finished the season with a total of 53 appearances.

Neuer started the next season and trained so well that he has victory for his team in the German Cup. As they won they reached the 2013 Champions League trophy.

They leave there with collecting 31 Bundesliga matches and about 20 more appearances scores in different tournaments across the season.

The 2014-15 season was very much better than before. So Manuel was also honored with the German Footballer of the Year award along with a place in the UEFA Team of the Year.

Manuel was selected to be a part of the national team and to experience an Asian tour. His international career starts there with the tour in 2009 when he was selected to be a part of the national team for the Asian tour.

He has also participated in World Cup 2010, as a goalkeeper of Germany.

In the 2014 World Cup, Manuel played exceptionally well and led his team straight into the finals, where the German team faced Argentina. Manuel had a lot of work to do in the finals and saved many goals.

Germany ended up winning the tournament and for his great performance as a goalkeeper, Manuel received the ‘Golden Glove’ award.

Manuel was selected for the 2016 Euro Cup. He played so well and didn’t let any team score goals against Germany fulfilling all his responsibilities of being a goalkeeper.

But unluckily Italy scored one and broke the streak. The match was stretched to a penalty round in which, Manuel made some sweet saves and led his team to victory, and was later named Man of the Match.

On October 23, 2021, his 300th Bundesliga victory came in his 447th Bundesliga game.

Likewise, he won the Bundesliga during the 2021-22 seasons with three games, after the season Manuel increased his contract with Bayern Munich till 2024.
