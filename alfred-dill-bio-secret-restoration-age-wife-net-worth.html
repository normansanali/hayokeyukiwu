Alfred Dill On Secret Restoration

Alfred Dill of Secret Restoration is a masterer tinkerer. According to History’s bio, “Alfred has been restoring antiques with gears, coin slots or strings since he was a teenager, and loves anything with a mechanism. He forages flea markets, antique shops, and even junkyards for items to revive—from vintage toy trains to coin-operated machinery and old radios to electric guitars.”

The bio concluded, “He’s also a talented musician and gets a huge satisfaction hearing the working sound his restorations make once the job is complete.”

Secret Restoration of History Channel is a reality TV series that followed a team of some of the nation’s most skilled and knowledgeable restorers and craftsmen who work together to fulfill people’s aspirations. As the title suggests, these experts repair and restore some significant family treasures and other priceless items in a secret facility. After giving their best effort, the artisans show the repaired things to unaware customers.

The artists in this reality program work with a variety of tools, including vintage pinball machines, hot rods, guitars, and ancient canons. The unique idea makes it a wholesome and informative viewing, with the restorers revealing secret elements of the ancient objects they work on and giving people some prized possessions.

Secret Restoration was reportedly filmed in Connecticut, in Bridgeport, Stamford, and Southington. The filming began in April 2022 and wrapped up in July. Other cast members joining her in the show are; Leah Bolden, PJ Burchett, David Dall, Sara Porter, and LaTasha “LA” Thym.
