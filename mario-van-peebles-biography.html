Mario Van Peebles: Professional Life, Career

Mario Van Peebles started his career playing a lead role in the short-lived detective show “Sonny Spoon”.

Afterward, Mario Van Peebles directed several episodes of the show “21 Jump Street”. Mario made his first film “New Jack City” in the year 1991.

Soon, he also directed “Posse” in 1993, “Panther” in 1995, and “Love Kills” in 1998. His guest appearance on the television show “Damages” made him more popular.

In total he has worked in forty -five films, and twenty-six television shows, and has directed twenty-five projects including films and episodes.

Some of his popular directed movies include Hard Luck (2006), Redemption Road (2010), Zero Hour (2013), Red Sky (2014), etc.
