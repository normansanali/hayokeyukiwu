Has Jo Cox Husband Remarried?

There is no proof to tell that Jo Cox’s husband has remarried. The pair were married from 2009 and had two children together until Jo’s death parted them.

When she met her husband, he was serving as an adviser on international development to Gordon Brown during Brown’s premiership. They met each other when Jo was working for Oxfam.

The happy family of four was divided when Jo was fatally shot and stabbed outside a library in Birstall, West Yorkshire. According to eyewitnesses, she was shot three times—once near the head—and stabbed multiple times.

A 77 years old local man was also killed while trying to fend off her attacker. Since her death, her husband has not got married and living his life with their children, Lejla and Cuillin.
