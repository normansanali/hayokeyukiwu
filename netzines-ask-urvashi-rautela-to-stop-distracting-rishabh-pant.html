Netzines Ask Urvashi Rautela To Stop Distracting Rishabh Pant

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Bollywood actress Urvashi Rautela is being brutally trolled on social media asking her to stop distracting the captain of IPL team Delhi Capitals, Rishabh Pant. The actress, who often makes headlines for her svelte fitness body and gym workouts, is catching attention for a bizarre reason.

Urvashi extended birthday wishes to cricketer Rishabh Pant on Twitter. Rishabh Pant’s birthday was on October 4 and the actress wished him a day late on October 5. While her gesture was really sweet to wish him, netizens couldn’t digest it because they were bothered with Urvashi’s rumoured relationship with Rishabh Pant in the past.

Urvashi had to bear the brunt of social media’s users’ wrath for having broken up with the cricketer in the past. For the unversed, Urvashi Rautela and Pant have reportedly dated in 2018. Later, the couple decided to call it quits and apparently, as a consequence of the break-up, Pant had blocked the actress on Twitter. And this did not go well with his fans, who now ask Urvashi to stay away from him.

“Pls don’t distract our boy before wc,” wrote a Twitter user. “Ynha bhi Block hogi lg rha,” wrote another. “He is one of the key players in t20 wc pls don’t disturb him,” tweeted yet another user. She soon became fodder for a meme-fest and netizens produced witty stuff in her name.

On the work front, Urvashi Rautela is busy with the Jio studios web series ‘Inspector Avinash’ opposite Randeep Hooda. She is also working on other projects such as a bilingual thriller Black Rose, and her Tamil debut with the 200 crores big-budget film ‘The Legend’ opposite Saravana. The actress has also signed a three-film contract with Jio Studios and T-Series.
