Hailey Bieber Is an It Girl in a Cozy Winter Sweater and Bike Shorts

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Hailey Bieber just wore an oversized winter sweater with no pants, and now we want to wear an oversized winter sweater with no pants.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The model stepped out for lunch with friends yesterday in Los Angeles looking like the ultimate It girl. Her sporty, cozy look consisted of a big zip-up turtleneck sweater in an array of warm autumn colors—cream, burnt orange, brown—paired with brown biker shorts.

For footwear, she opted for chunky white, green, and orange sneakers, and white ankle socks. She finished the look in the most Hailey Bieber way: with a green baseball cap, gold hoops, and tiny brown-and-gold sunglasses.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Putting an elevated spin on her casual yet highly curated street style moment, Bieber also carried a chic leather handbag in a rosy sand tone with gold hardware from Saint Laurent by Anthony Vaccarello.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Bieber met up with friends Lori Harvey and Justine Skye. The three were photographed grabbing a casual bite to eat at a restaurant.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The Rhode founder's latest off-duty look undoubtedly resembles the enviable style of Princess Diana. In an interview for Harper's BAZAAR's September cover story, the supermodel discussed how she sources inspiration from the late royal—not only from her style, but also in the way she carried herself in the world.

"I was really inspired by the fact that she was the most-looked-at woman in the world at that time, of all time, and she did what she wanted with her style," Bieber said of Lady Di. "She really expressed herself through her style despite being in the position she was in."
