How do you spell front porch?

Correct spelling for the English word “front–porch” is [fɹˈʌntpˈɔːt͡ʃ], [fɹˈʌntpˈɔːt‍ʃ], [f_ɹ_ˈʌ_n_t_p_ˈɔː_tʃ] (IPA phonetic alphabet).

What does porch mean?

1 : a covered area adjoining an entrance to a building and usually having a separate roof. 2 obsolete : portico.

What is the right spelling of porch?

Correct spelling for the English word “porch” is [pˈɔːt͡ʃ], [pˈɔːt‍ʃ], [p_ˈɔː_tʃ] (IPA phonetic alphabet).

How do you spell porch in English?

noun. an exterior appendage to a building, forming a covered approach or vestibule to a doorway. a veranda.

Why is it called a porch?

The word “porch” derives from the Old French word porche, from the Latin root word for passage. Early French and Spanish Colonial houses featured verandas, or porches, that featured covered roofs and often wrapped around the residence.

What does veranda mean?

: a usually roofed open gallery or portico attached to the exterior of a building.
