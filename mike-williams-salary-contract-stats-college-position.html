Mike Williams Salary, Contract, Stats, College, Position

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Mike Williams is an American football wide receiver for the Los Angeles Chargers of the National Football League.

The Los Angeles Chargers selected Mike Williams in the first round with the seventh overall pick in the 2017 NFL Draft. Mike Williams made his professional regular season debut in Week 6 of the 2017 season.

Mike Williams Salary

Mike Williams receives an average annual salary of $20,000,000 from the Los Angeles Chargers.

Mike Williams Contract

Mike Williams signed a 3 year, $60,000,000 contract with the Los Angeles Chargers on March 8, 2022, including a $21,000,000 signing bonus, an average annual salary of $20,000,000 and $40,000,000 in guarantees.

Mike Williams Stats

Mike Williams’s career NFL statistics as of Week 1, 2022 include receptions of 229, receiving yards: 3,672, receiving touchdowns: 26, rushing yards: 31 and rushing touchdowns: 1.

Mike Williams College

Mike Williams played college football at Clemson. As a true freshman, he made three starts in all 13 games.

Mike Williams Position

Mike Williams plays the position of a football wide receiver.
