Bob Penny My Cousin Vinny: What Role Did Bob Penny Play In My Cousin Vinny?

Publish date: 2024-08-02

The late Bob Penny was an Alabama college professor and actor who entered the public eye with some of his roles in movies like “Forrest Gump” and “Sweet Home Alabama,”.

The late Bob Penny was born in Anniston, Alabama in 1935 and died on the 25th of December 2022 at 87.

His parents were the late William Burbank Penny and Agnes Marion Jones Penny. Bob Penny grew up in North Carolina after spending his childhood period in Jacksonville, AL, where his father was a Presbyterian minister.

Bob Penny is an alumnus of the University of Wofford College, Columbia University, North Carolina, and the University of Alabama at Tuscaloosa.

Per his career history, he did over 40 character and leading roles in dramatic productions throughout the Southeast. Bob Penny is Survived by his brother, William Earl Penny (Betty), sister, Jean Marion Yount, six nieces, and one nephew.

What Role Did Bob Penny Play In My Cousin Vinny?

Bob Penny played the role of Juror number 2 in ‘My Cousin Vinny’.
