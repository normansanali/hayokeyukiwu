Biography

Lana Condor was born in Vietnam on May 11, 1997. Vietnamese-American actress who made her film debut in the 2016 blockbuster X-Men: Apocalypse as Jubilee and was then cast in the drama Patriots Day. She later starred in Netflix’s To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before and Syfy’s Deadly Class as the main character, Saya. Other lead roles include Alita: Battle Angel and Girl’s Night. She and Melissa Benoist were both casts in Patriot’s Day. She trained as a dancer at three different facilities, including the Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater in New York City. She later took acting classes at the New York Film Academy.
