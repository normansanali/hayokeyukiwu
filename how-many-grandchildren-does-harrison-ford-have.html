How Many Grandchildren Does Harrison Ford Have?

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Before he skyrocketed to fame, Harrison Ford was married to Mary Marquardt, with whom he had two sons, Ben and Willard. The marriage ended in 1979, according to The Guardian, after Ford had an affair with Carrie Fisher while filming "Star Wars." Ford's sons were adolescents at the time and are still close to their father.

Ford's oldest son Ben (aged 53) posted a picture of himself with his dad on Twitter in 2019, saying, "My dad or pops (newly adapted) ... taught me resiliency as a young father and later helped give me the courage to pursue my dreams." Ben has given Ford two grandsons, Ethan and Waylon. Inspired by his mother's cooking, Ben became a celebrity chef with his own restaurant in L.A. and the author of a cookbook, according to the Daily Mail.

Meanwhile, 52-year-old Willard has also given Ford two grandchildren, Eliel and Guiliana. Willard leads a far more private life than his brother, but according to his LinkedIn profile, he is a partner at a bicycle shop and club called The Super Fred. He also guides bicycle tours in "Mexico, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, Japan, France, and other countries," according to his profile.
