Love on the Spectrum Season 2 Release date : When will it Air?

Love on the Spectrum aired on ABC and iView on 19th November 2019. After the five episodes became the most-watched ones, Netflix jumped in. Netflix helped the show increase its audience, and it became widely popular after Netflix released it on 22nd July 2020. We have good news for you and also bad news about the status of season 2. The good news is Northern Pictures (Production company) already started the production of the second season. The bad news is that there is no official release date, and there are also high chances that the production might have stopped due to COVID-19. However, a second season is inevitable, and we are expecting it to release in 2021, maybe in the mid of year. Netflix will release the show after a month of its release in Australia. 

Season two of Love on the Spectrum will air on Netflix on September 21, possibly around the same time Bachelor in Paradise closes, which is fantastic news for romantic docuseries fans.

Love on the Spectrum Season 2 Plot : what is it about?

Love is the most beautiful feeling one can experience in their life. However, some are lucky; some are not that lucky to find love easily. There are instances when love doesn’t come easy, and even the strongest person can feel dejection. Even the most extroverted person can face difficulty expressing their feelings and think about the people who have ASD (Autism Spectrum Disorder). This show documents the journey of people who fight their differences and fears to find the partner that completes them. It shows how they learned to express their feelings with the help and support of the power of love.
