Gaby Hoffmann Gives Birth to Baby Girl With Boyfriend Chris Dapkins

Publish date: 2024-09-29

A Girls' girl! Gaby Hoffmann became a mom last month when she gave birth to her first child, a baby daughter, her manager confirms to Us Weekly exclusively.

"Gaby had a daughter, Rosemary, with boyfriend Chris Dapkins, born at home on November 19," the manager tells Us. "Everyone is healthy and doing great."

The Girls guest star, 32, first revealed her baby bump at a screening of her movie Obvious Child back in June. A source told Us at the time that she and cinematographer Dapkins were "thrilled" and "doing very well."

A former child star, Hoffmann is best known for movies including 1989's Field of Dreams, 1993's Sleepless in Seattle, and 1995's Now and Then. More recently, she played Caroline Sackler on HBO's Girls and had a role in the 2013 indie Crystal Fairy. She also appears in the Reese Witherspoon movie Wild and stars in Amazon's acclaimed series Transparent.
