Biography:

Jena Malone was born on November 21, 1984 in Lake Tahoe, Nevada, USA. She developed her interest in acting after watching her mother, who was involved in community theatre.

In 1996, she made her professional acting debut in the film Bastard Out of Carolina. She gained an Independent Spirit Award nomination for Best Debut Performance for her role.

The following year, she was nominated for a Golden Globe for Best Actress for her role in the television movie Hope.

In 2012, she landed the role of Johanna Mason in the sequels to Hunger Games, The Hunger Games: Catching Fire and The Hunger Games: Mockingjay.
