JALEEL WHITE AND DAUGHTER CLOSE OUT SPRING BREAK IN THE MOUNTAINS

Publish date: 2024-09-28

Jaleel White and his daughter, Samaya White, enjoyed spring break in the mountains. The celebrity father took to Instagram on Tuesday (Apr. 12) to commemorate the end of the time of rest and regeneration.

“And just that fast … Spring Break 2022 is over,” Jaleel told his social media fans. “This is the face my daughter makes when she ’bout to ask me for something,” White said of Samaya smiling at the camera without showing her teeth. “Even though she won’t smile with her braces,” Jaleel added. “Please tell this child she’s just as beautiful and we’ve all been there,” the celebrity father requested of his fans.

Jaleel and his daughter enjoyed mountain bike riding while vacationing for the spring. “Found out the hard way the importance of riding mountain bikes only when they have that little wheel fender thingy over the wheels,” White told his social media fans. “We all look like a flock of birds intentionally targeted us for the purpose of sh*tting down our backs,” the actor said.

Samaya White is Jaleel White’s daughter with actress Bridget Hardy. The teen is in middle school and learning about the wonders of adolescence. It was just the other week that Jaleel took to Instagram to talk about the April Fools joke Samaya played on him at the top of the month.

“So I decide to bring ice cream home as a surprise,” White began the story. “I look down and see Ms. Seventh Grade has gotten herself a colorful array of video vixen acrylic nails,” Jaleel said of his daughter’s appearance. “And we TALKED ABOUT THIS and how it was NOT allowed,” Jaleel shared along with an angry emoji.

“I’m immediately HEATED but I stay chill. Why did you do this behind my back? I just wanna know what made you do it? And how would you handle it if you were me and I was the daughter here? I’m super chill about it. No yelling at all,” Jaleel explained. “I see her and my gal glancing at each other… these foolz (sic) have taped FAKE Acrylic nails to her fingernails just to see what I would do ??????? #wellplayed.”
