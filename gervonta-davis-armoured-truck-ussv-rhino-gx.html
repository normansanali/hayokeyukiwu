Gervonta Davis arrived for last fight in $300,000 armoured truck that weighs 10,000lbs

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Gervonta Davis arrived in style for his last fight against Hector Luis Garcia at the start of this year.

In January, the 28-year-old American star put on a show in front of just under 20,000 fans in Washington DC as he stopped his Dominican opponent, who said he couldn't see after a torrid eighth round.

Prior to the fight though, Tank caught eyes with his arrival as he was dropped off in his gigantic custom USSV Rhino GX.

US Specialty Vehicles manufacture the truck which is based on the Ford F-450 and boasted as one of the largest luxury SUVs available on the market.

The armoured vehicle weighs close to 10,000lbs and costs in the region of $300,000 (£248,000).

It can seat up to seven people and is equipped to attack various terrains with an all-wheel-drive system powered by either a 6.7-litre diesel or 6.8-litre V8 engine.

Despite its appearance, the Rhino GX's exterior isn't actually bulletproof, though it can be up-armoured to become so.

It is 18ft 9ins (5.7m) long, 8ft (2.4m) wide and 7ft 4ins (2.2m) tall.

Back in 2021, Champion Motoring posted pictures on Instagram showing the custom Rhino GX they had made for Davis.

This version was tan coloured while the one he appeared in on Saturday night was pure black.

It's unclear whether or not this is the same car with a new paint job or if Davis has since purchased a second of the same model.

Either way, the version posted on Instagram in 2021 had a 40-inch TV mounted in the back.

The giant screen was fitted to the partition wall separating the driver from the passengers, though they can still communicate through the car's intercom.

It also has a super-modern touch screen entertainment system which would have no doubt kept Davis happy on his way to and from the fight.

He may well choose to take the same mode of transport when he travels to his fight with Ryan Garcia on Saturday night.
