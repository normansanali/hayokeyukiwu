Pregnant Emma Roberts Shows Baby Bump in Bathing Suit: Pic

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Strapless suit! Pregnant Emma Roberts gave a glimpse of her baby bump on Monday, September 7.

“Happy Labor Day,” the actress, 29, captioned an Instagram Story selfie while wearing a navy J. Crew one-piece patterned with red flowers, as well as matching Mulberry sunglasses.

Us Weekly broke the news in June that the American Horror Story alum is expecting her first child with her boyfriend, Garrett Hedlund.

“[They are] surprised, shocked and happy at the same time, and so are their families,” a source exclusively told Us the following month. “They know the sex of the baby but are keeping it private for now. They haven’t picked out any names yet.”

The Scream Queens alum confirmed her pregnancy news last month, debuting her baby bump in an August Instagram slideshow. “Me … and my two favorite guys,” the New York native captioned photos with Hedlund, 36, revealing the sex of their baby-to-be.

The couple started dating in March 2019 after Roberts split from her on-again, off-again fiancé, Evan Peters. As for the Tron: Legacy star, Hedlund previously dated Kirsten Dunst before they called it quits in 2016.
